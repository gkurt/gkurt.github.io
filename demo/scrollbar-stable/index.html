Scrollbar-stable padding

Classic scrollbars shift content 11px to the left when they appear. scrollbar-gutter: stable fixes the shift by always reserving the gutter — but that wastes space when no scrolling is needed.

The trick: use @container scroll-state() on the scroller to detect when it's actually scrollable, and shave 11px off the right padding only in that state.

Requires container-type: scroll-state (Chromium 133+, Safari 26+).
